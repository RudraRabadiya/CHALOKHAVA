Our Story

The Taste of Gujarat,
Right in Your Heart

Born in the heart of Surat's old city, Chalo Khava brings the warmth of Gujarati hospitality to every single plate. Our recipes have been passed down through three generations — crafted with love, spice, and the soul of a home kitchen.

From the crunch of fresh Khandvi to the richness of Dal Dhokli, we serve beloved Gujarati classics alongside multi-cuisine favourites — all made fresh, every morning.
